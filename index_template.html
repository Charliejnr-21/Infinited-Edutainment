Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how InfinitEd works and how we monetize.

How exactly does the AI turn my study notes into songs or anime? +
Our platform operates as an advanced AI orchestrator. First, we parse your uploaded document or pasted text to extract the core academic concepts and definitions. Next, we use fine-tuned Large Language Models to structure those concepts into highly catchy rhyming lyrics or dramatic animation scripts. Finally, we route these to industry-leading generative audio and video AI models (like Suno, ElevenLabs, and Runway) to synthesize the final master media file!
What is the difference between the Free Tier and Premium Tier? +
The Free Tier is designed as a teaser to let you experience the magic of musical study notes. It permits up to 100 characters per submission, focuses exclusively on the Song/Rap generator, and offers 3-5 monthly generations across 20+ music genres. The Premium Tier ($9.99/mo) unlocks the entire suite: full access to the Anime & Animation engine (20+ art styles), the Immersive Novel builder (20+ themes), unlimited character inputs (allowing you to upload entire textbook chapters), and priority generation speeds.
Can I choose any music genre in the Free Tier? +
Yes! As requested in our core platform design, all 20+ music genres—including 90s Boom Bap Rap, Lo-Fi Chill Beats, Synthwave Pop, Cyberpunk EDM, Afrobeats, and Acoustic Rock—are fully accessible in both the Free Tier and the Premium Tier.
What file formats can I upload? +
You can paste raw text directly into the studio, or upload standard document formats including PDF, Microsoft Word (.docx), and PowerPoint (.pptx) slides. Our backend parsers automatically clean and structure the text before processing.
